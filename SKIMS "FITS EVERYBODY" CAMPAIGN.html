Campaign 2024

Fits Everybody.
Because every body fits.

SKIMS needed to reinforce its position as the inclusive shapewear brand while expanding into loungewear. The challenge: communicate that "Fits Everybody" isn't just a tagline, it's a promise.

01

The Challenge

Drive 40% growth in new customer acquisition while maintaining brand premiumness. Reach sizes XXS-5X demographics authentically without tokenism. Target women 25-45 who have felt excluded by traditional shapewear brands. They want solutions, not shame. Comfort, not compromise.

Key Insight: "Every body" is grammatically incorrect but emotionally perfect. It acknowledges bodies as individual, not universal.
