Campaign Strategy

The Brief

The Challenge

Launch Rhode's Barrier Restore Cream in an oversaturated skincare market where every brand claims to "repair" skin. Stand out without making unrealistic promises.

The Insight

Women don't want perfect skin. They want skin that can handle their actual life: the stress, the travel, the forgotten SPF, the wine, the late nights.

Target Audience

28-40 year old women who've tried everything. They're skeptical of skincare promises but hopeful enough to try "one more thing" if it sounds honest.

The Strategy

Position skincare as life support, not life changing. We're not selling transformation, we're selling resilience. Skin that can live your life with you.
